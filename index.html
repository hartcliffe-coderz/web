About us

Hartcliffe Coderz provides free coding classes to young people in Hartcliffe. Our mission is to empower youth with the skills and knowledge to excel in the digital age while tackling anti-social behaviour in the community.

We believe that coding is an essential skill that can open doors to a world of possibilities, and we provide a fun and supportive environment for learning and growth. Our classes are suitable for complete beginners and are taught by industry professionals using team work and hands-on projects.

Learn more about our free coding camps for summer 2023 below. If you can't find the information you're looking for on this page, please contact us today. We'd be happy to answer any questions you may have and help you get started on your coding journey.

Web Development

In this summer camp you will learn to design and develop modern websites using HTML and CSS. Our expert mentors will guide you through each step of the web development process, helping you create user-friendly and responsive websites that are optimised for speed and accessibility.

Through hands-on projects and challenges, you will gain a solid foundation in web development and build essential skills in design, layout, and typography. You'll have access to all the necessary tools and equipment, including laptops and development software, so you can make the most of your learning experience.

Our web development summer camp is completely free of charge and provides a fun and supportive environment for young people to explore their interests, learn new skills, and make new friends. No prior experience in coding is necessary as we are starting with the basics.

Game Development

Scratch is used as one of the tools for our game development summer camp. It is a visual programming language that is designed specifically for young people, making it an ideal platform for introducing beginners to the world of coding and game development.

Scratch allows you to create games by dragging and dropping blocks of code, rather than typing out lines of text. This makes it easy to experiment with different programming concepts and create complex games without needing to memorise complex syntax. For those of you who are up for a challenge, we will explore together the programming language Python.

You will learn how to create characters, animate movements, design levels, and add sound effects and music to create an immersive gaming experience. You'll have access to all the necessary tools and equipment, including laptops and development software, so you can make the most of your learning experience.
